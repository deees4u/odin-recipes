Tomato Fried Eggs

This is my favorite dish! It's also very easy to make.

Source from icook.tw

Look how colorful it is! I'm dripping just by looking at this picture. haha

You like it? Look delicious? Here's the how to make it.

Ingredients

  • Eight cherry tomatos
  • Three Eggs
  • Two pieces of garlic
  • Your preferred amount of green onions
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • One tea spoon of Sugar
  • Ketchup (Not required)

Steps

  1. Put oil. Heat it until you see the smoke (So it's hot enough and egss won't stick to the wok)
  2. Put the eggs in. Fried it until half-cooked. Take it out
  3. Lower the fire. Put the garlic in. Fried it for 30 seconds
  4. Put in the half-chopped tomatos and seasonings, Fried it until you see the color turns red
  5. Now put in the eggs. Fried it for 30 seconds to 1 minute
  6. Take out everything and just sprinkle the dish with the green onions
  7. Say Tada!
