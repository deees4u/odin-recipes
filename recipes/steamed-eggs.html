Steamed Eggs

This is also my favorite dish!

Source from Made With Lau

Yes, it's egg again! I love eggggggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups warm water
  • 2 green onions
  • 0.5 tsp salt
  • 0.5 tsp chicken bouillon
  • 0.5 tbsp oil
  • white pepper (to taste)

Steps

  1. Chop the green onion
  2. Boil water in a wok. Place a steamer rack in the wok, and place your bowl on top of the rack.
  3. Start mixing eggs. Crack all four eggs in to a bowl, add salt, chicken bouillon, and oil. Start mixing the eggs with a fork or a pair of chopstick.
  4. Add warm water and mix with the eggs
  5. Make sure the water is boiling. Add the mixed eggs into the steaming bowl.
  6. Leave the stove on high heat until the water boil again. It should take 1 to 4 minutes. Then lower the heat to a simmer, and cook for another 8 minutes.
  7. Remove the lid, add green oinions and white pepper to the bowl.
  8. Say Tada!!
